What is meant as climate change?

Climate change refers to long-term shiifts in temperatures
and weather patterns. Such shifts can be natural, due to
changes in the sun's activity or large volcanic erruptions or
human activities like burning of fossil fuels, cutting down of
trees and lack of proper waste management.
