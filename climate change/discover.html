What is Climate Change?

Climate Change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns.
Such shifts can be natural,due to changes in the sun's activity or large volcanic
eruptions. But snice the 1800s, human activities have been the main driver of
cliamte change. Primarily due to the burning of fossil fuels like coal, oil and gas.

Why is it Important to Know About Climate Change?

Climate change is a pressing issue with global implications, demanding attention for
several critical reasons. It threatens ecosystem, infrastructure, and communities,
leading to increased frequency and intensity of extreme weather events, like floods,
drought, and storms. The changing climate also impacts global food supplies,
agricultural productivity, and water resources.Futher more,climate change
exacerbates risks to human health, livelihoods, and economic growth.
